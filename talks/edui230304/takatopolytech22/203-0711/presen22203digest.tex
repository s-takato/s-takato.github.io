$\phantom{(e^x)'}=\dlim_{z\to x}\bunsuu{e^{x}e^{z-x}-e^x}{z-x}=\dlim_{z\to x}\bunsuu{e^x(e^{z-x}-1)}{z-x}$\vspace{2mm}\\
